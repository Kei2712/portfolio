About Me

Passionate and Dedicated

Since my early years, a profound love for games, movies, and TV series has fuelled my creative journey. Guided by my father's introduction to gaming, I delved into the world of fan art, using it as a canvas to hone my drawing skills. It quickly became evident that the intersection of art and gaming was the path for me. My passion for both traditional and digital art, from art studies to crafting original characters, has been a constant source of joy and fulfilment. Today, I take pride in creating art that resonates with my interests and passions as well as contributing to an Indie game project with friends, where I'm able to channel my creativity into crafting compelling creature concepts, further nurturing my passion for art.
